The Vineyard

Our winery is located in the Southern Hemisphere, in one of the southernmost areas of the planet under a starry sky, in Maipu, Mendoza, Argentina. Our wines are produced from a 70 hectares vineyard, 800 meters above sea level, next to the Andes Mountains. This area has a wide temperature range and rocky soil.

The vineyards have 15 year old vines that are irrigated with water coming from the snowmelt of the Andes mountains which we take from underground using pumps and drip irrigation systems.

We work exhaustively on vineyards throughout the year to achieve the best grape quality in each harvest. We carry out a continuous pruning of buds and thinning of bunches to reach a deeper concentration of aromas and colors.

Harvest is done in February and March, which is our harvesting season called “vendimia” in Argentina. This is a very special time for us, we dedicate lots of efforts to hand pick the grapes and make an exclusive selection for achieving high quality wines.

We produce different types of wines: generic red wine (which is a mix of local red grapes), varietals such as Malbec, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Chardonnay, Petit Verdot, Cabernet Franc. We also make blends of these grapes and turn them into Reserva and Gran Reserva wines.

Our mission is to produce unique wines. Our goal is to take the best of our soil, sun and nature and make high quality wines where all our natural resources can be sensed in a glass of wine.

Winemaking
Process

“Despalillado” process

It consists of separating the grape from the “scrape” that is the herbaceous structure of the bunch, then it goes through rollers where the grain breaks so that the must is released.

Cold Maceration

Once the grinding is done, a cold maceration is carried out for 24 hours at 15 °C, this favors the extraction of color and aromas.

Alcoholic Fermentation and final process

Once the alcoholic fermentation is finished, the we separate the wine from the “hat” to begin a malolactic fermentation. It is the transformation of malic acid into lactic acid, this is done by a bacteria that is naturally found in the grape itself and therefore also in the freshly fermented wine.

Once this fermentation is finished, the wine is filtered and then it is divided into fresh varietals or reserve wines (this is defined before the harvest since they are worked from the vine plants), and they are sent to barrels of French oak of first and second use for an approximate time of 12 months depending on the type of wine we want to achieve.

Terroir

Soil composition is rocky with little organic matter, determining excellent drainage rates in summer. Scarce rainfall produces healthy grapes.

Intense sunlight exposure and low temperatures at night, that favor grape ripeness, achieving the right balance between acidity and sweetness.

The Valley is considered one of the major wine regions of the world, it is by this and by the natural beauty of this land that we chose it as a place for our project.

The Wines

01

Valle Austral Estate Malbec

Intense purple red color. Red fruit notes, sweet, spicy, intense aromas. The palate is soft, velvety and fresh, with a very good balance.

Technical Information

Varietal • 100% Malbec

Alcohol • 13.6% vol.

02

Valle Austral Malbec Reserva

Great intensity with violet hues. Aromas of various fruits and minerals, with notes of caramel, vanilla and chocolate from its passage through oak barrels.

It is a wine that enters the mouth with the characteristic smoothness of the variety. Balanced with great structure and with its presence of soft tannins.

Aging: 10 months in first-use French oak barrels.

Technical Information

Varietal • 100% Malbec

Alcohol • 13.3% vol.

03

Valle Austral Petit Verdot

With manually selected grapes, we make this blend of Cabernet Franc and Petit Verdot, two varieties that occur from exceptional way in the region from El Paraiso, in Mendoza.

Intense red in color, with violet reflections. A combination of aromas of ripe red fruits and spices. A structured and full-bodied wine, with round tannins that give it a persistent finish.

Technical Information

Varietal • 80% Cabernet Franc, 20% Petit Verdot

Alcohol • 13.6% vol.

04

Valle Austral Chardonnay

Intense yellow color with greenish tones. It presents aromas of tropical fruits, almonds, accompanied by notes of vanilla, pear and pineapple.

Balanced, fresh with a long and delicate finish. Serve at a temperature between 6ºC and 8ºC.

Technical Information

Varietal • 100% Chardonnay

Alcohol • 13.4% vol.
